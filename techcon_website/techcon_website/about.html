Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of passionate developers and has since grown into a global technology conference. Over the years, it has welcomed thousands of attendees and featured hundreds of innovative talks and workshops.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire and empower technology professionals and enthusiasts by fostering knowledge-sharing, innovation, and inclusive networking opportunities.

Notable Past Speakers

Jane Smith

Jane is a pioneer in artificial intelligence and was one of our keynote speakers in 2021. She shared her insights on ethical AI and the future of machine learning.

Rahul Patel

Rahul, a leading voice in cloud architecture, joined TechCon 2022 to discuss scaling distributed systems and the evolution of cloud-native technologies.

Lina Zhao

Lina delivered a powerful talk in 2023 about the importance of cybersecurity resilience in modern organizations.
